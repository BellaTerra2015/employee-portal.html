⏱️ BusyBusy Instructions

📱

BusyBusy is how Bella Terra tracks your time. All hours must be submitted by 8:00 AM Monday. Use the guides below to clock in, clock out, take breaks, switch jobs, and fix any mistakes. If you have questions after reviewing these instructions, contact Emily.

🟢 Clocking In & Out

1
How to Clock In
▼
  1. 1Open BusyBusy and make sure you are on your Dashboard.
  2. 2Press the Clock In button.
  3. 3Choose your Project from the list.
  4. 4Choose your Cost Code.
  5. 5Check that the wage shown is correct:
    • Government job → Prevailing Wage (unless it's a maintenance job)
    • Residential job → Base Wage
  6. 6If your jobsite is not listed or you are unsure what job you're on, press 140 and add detailed notes — what you did, your address, and any other key details.
  7. 7Press Done — the clock will start running.
🎬

2
How to Clock Out
▼
  1. 1Open BusyBusy and press the red Clock Out button.
  2. 2Answer the "Were you hurt?" prompt.
  3. 3Confirm whether your timecard is correct:
    • Yes, it's correct → press Yes and continue.
    • No, it's not correct (e.g. forgot to clock out earlier) → press No, add the correct details in the notes, then text or email Emily with what needs to be fixed. You can also fix it yourself — see the Fix Timecards section below.
  4. 4Press Submit in the top-right corner. You're all clocked out.
📅
All hours must be submitted by 8:00 AM Monday. To submit: review your hours and sign your timecard. If you miss the deadline, contact Emily with your corrections and update BusyBusy accordingly.

☕ Breaks & Switching Jobs

3
How to Take a Break
▼
  1. 1While clocked in, you will see a Take a Break button — press it.
  2. 2Your job will stay logged during the break, and a break timer will run.
  3. 3When you are done with your break, press the End Break button.
  4. 4Your original job clock resumes automatically.
🎬

How to Switch Jobs
▼
  1. 1While clocked in, press the Switch Jobs button.
  2. 2Select the new project and cost code.
  3. 3Press Done — your time will immediately start logging under the new job.

🛠️ Fixing Timecards

⚠️
IF YOU MAKE ADJUSTMENTS AFTER SUBMITTING YOUR TIME (AFTER MONDAY @ 8:00 AM) YOU MUST NOTIFY EMILY — email, text, or a sticky note. If you don't, your time will not be corrected and you will not be paid accurately.
5
Forgot to Clock Out
▼
  1. 1Open the app to the Clock screen.
  2. 2Press the red Clock Out button.
  3. 3Answer the "Were you hurt?" prompt.
  4. 4Press No, it was not correct and type in what happened — for example: "Forgot to clock out, should have been 4:30 PM."
  5. 5Press Submit.
  6. 6Go to your Entries for that day.
  7. 7Press the ✏️ pencil icon to edit the entry.
  8. 8Change the end time to what it should have been and save.
🎬

6
Forgot to Clock In
▼
  1. 1Open BusyBusy and press Clock In, selecting the job as normal.
  2. 2Once you press OK and the clock starts, go to your Entries.
  3. 3Press on the current entry.
  4. 4Press the ✏️ pencil icon.
  5. 5Update the start time to when you actually began work.
  6. 6Save.

Adjust Time on a Past Entry
▼
  1. 1Press the Entries tab.
  2. 2Select the day you need to edit.
  3. 3Press the ✏️ pencil icon.
  4. 4Adjust the time to be correct.
  5. 5Press Submit.

Add a Missing Day or Missing Time
▼
  1. 1Press the Timecards button at the bottom of the screen.
  2. 2At the top, press the + next to the word "Time Card".
  3. 3Fill in all applicable information as you normally would when clocking in.
  4. 4Press Submit.
