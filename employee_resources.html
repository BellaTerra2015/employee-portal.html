Bella Terra — Break & Meal Period Acknowledgment

Rest Breaks (Paid): 10 minutes for every 4 hours worked. Cannot be waived to leave early.

Meal Breaks (Unpaid): 30 minutes when working more than 5 hours.

  • Take your assigned breaks as scheduled
  • Immediately notify your supervisor if you cannot take a break
